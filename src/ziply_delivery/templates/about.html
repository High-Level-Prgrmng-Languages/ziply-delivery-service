Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of logistics experts and technology innovators, Ziply Delivery emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make package delivery as seamless and reliable as sending a text message.

Starting with just 5 delivery vehicles in a single city, we've grown into a global network spanning over 150 countries. Our journey has been driven by relentless innovation, customer-first thinking, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we're proud to serve millions of customers worldwide, from individual consumers to Fortune 500 companies, all while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that defined our early days.
