Frequently Asked Questions

Tracking & Delivery

How do I track my package?

Use our tracking page with your tracking number (format: TRK123456789) to get real-time updates. You'll receive SMS and email notifications at key delivery milestones.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is 1-2 days, and overnight delivery arrives the next business day. International shipping varies by destination (5-15 business days).

What if my package is delayed?

We'll notify you immediately of any delays. You can also check real-time updates on our tracking page. For urgent matters, contact our support team for priority assistance.

Shipping & Pricing

How much does shipping cost?

Pricing depends on package size, weight, destination, and delivery speed. Use our shipping calculator for instant quotes. We offer competitive rates with no hidden fees.

What items can I ship?

We ship most items including documents, electronics, clothing, and household goods. Prohibited items include hazardous materials, perishables, and illegal substances.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes! We provide free insurance up to $100 value. Additional coverage is available for high-value items at competitive rates.

Account & Technical

Do I need an account to ship?

No account required for one-time shipments. However, creating an account lets you save addresses, track multiple packages, and access shipping history.

How do I change my delivery address?

Contact us before your package ships to modify the delivery address. Once in transit, address changes may incur additional fees depending on the new location.

Can I schedule a pickup?

Yes! Schedule pickups during checkout or contact us to arrange. We offer same-day pickup in major cities and next-day pickup in most areas.

Popular Help Topics

Package Not Found

If tracking shows no results, verify your tracking number format. Contact us if the issue persists - we'll locate your package immediately.

Shipping Labels

Download and print shipping labels from your confirmation email. Ensure labels are clear and securely attached to avoid delivery delays.

Refunds & Claims

Lost or damaged packages are covered by our guarantee. File claims within 30 days with photos and documentation for fast resolution.

International Shipping

We ship to 150+ countries with customs handling included. Check destination restrictions and required documentation before shipping.
